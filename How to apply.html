Applying for Online Admissions After Entry Test

  • To apply click here. http://admission.pucit.edu.pk
  • Please create Separate Accounts for Applying for Admissions to Degree Programs at Old/New Campuses.
  • Choose the (BS/M.Sc) degree program and campus to which you want to apply
  • Rs: 100 will be charged for each degree program, we highly recommended that you apply in all degree programs. However, final choice is yours
  • There are two buttons at the bottom of the Admission Form namely SAVE and FINALIZE.
    1. After providing all the required information, click on the SAVE button to save your information. Once saved, you can still update your provided information.
    2. Click on the FINALIZE button only when you are ready to submit the application. Once the application form is finalized, you will NOT be able to edit your information.
  • Once the Admission Form has been finalized, click on Challan Form. Click on DOWNLOAD CHALLAN FORM button to download the challan form in PDF format. Once downloaded, take its print and deposit the challan form in your nearest HBL Branch. Keep the depositor’s copy of your challan form for your personal record.
  • After filling your form, Get the print out of your Admission Form and keep it for your personal record.
  • The Admission Form fee is neither refundable nor transferable.
