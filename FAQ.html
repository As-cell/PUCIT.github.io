Frequently Asked Question Regarding Admission in PUCIT
Question 1: When does PUCIT offer admission to its BS, M.Sc. M.Phil. &PhD Programs?

Answer: PUCIT offers admission to all undergraduate programs for Fall Semester only. The cycle opens in June/July. Exact dates are announced through print and electronic media (newspaper, PUCIT website and social media).

Question 2: Can I receive a waiver for the PUCIT Entrance Test?

Answer: No. Without appearing in entrance test, you are not eligible to apply for admission at PUCIT.

Question 3: Does PUCIT consider standardized tests for admission (NTS, SAT, etc.)?

Answer: No

Question 4: Where is the PUCIT Entrance Test held?

Answer: The PUCIT Entrance Test is held at BISE halls, Examination Halls University of the Punjab, or on Campus. The information of venue is clearly mentioned on the entrance test roll no. slip of the applicant who wishes to appear in PUCIT entrance test.

Question 5: What documents do I bring at the test center?

    Answer: Bring the following original documents at the test center:

  1. Original PUCIT Entrance Test Roll Number Slip
  2. One of the following Original Picture Identity:
    • CNIC
    • Matric/Intermediate result card with picture
    • Driving License

    Note:Photocopies of the above mentioned documents are not accepted. Form-B is also not acceptable as it does not have picture on it.

Question 6: What are the consequences if I bring my PUCIT Entrance Test Roll Number Slip only and not my original picture identity?

Answer: You will not be allowed to take the Entrance Test.

Question 7: Can I bring any other thing in the test center?

    Answer: In addition to the above mentioned documents which prove your identity, you can only bring a ball point to take the test and write answers of the questions. Any other document is not allowed in the exam hall including the following:

  1. A blank paper to solve your test.
  2. Any helping material
  3. Any document other than those mentioned in question 5.
Question 8: Can I bring mobile phone, laptop, hand bag or calculator to the exam center?

Answer: No, you are not allowed to bring mobile phones, laptop, hand bags and calculators in the exam hall. You are also not allowed to bring any kind of weapon to the exam center.

Question 9: What are the consequences if I bring the things mentioned in 8 above?

Answer: If you found in possession of any of the above mentioned things in 8, your test will be cancelled and you will not be able to apply for admission to any of the academic programs at PUCIT.

Question 10: Can I take the entrance test twice?

Answer: No.

Question 11: Can I be allowed for the re-take entrance test if I miss it for any reason?

Answer: No. You are not allowed to re-take the entrance test if you miss it once. You can apply for the entrance test next year.

Question 12: Is the test held in different shifts?

Answer: Yes. Only BS Entrance Test is held in multiple shifts. M.Sc., M.Phil., and PhD Entrance Tests are held in single shift only. The shift, in which the applicant has been scheduled to appear, is mentioned on the Roll Number Slip.

Question 13: Can I be allowed to appear in the other shift if I miss my actual slot of entrance test accidently?

Answer: No. You are not allowed to appear in the other shift of the entrance test if you miss your own. You will apply for a new admission next year.

Question 14: How is the undergraduate merit calculated?

Answer: 70% is the Academic Record score, and 30% is the PUCIT entrance test score.

Question 15: How is the graduate merit calculated?

Answer: 40% is the Academic Record score, 50% is the PUCIT entrance test score, and 10% is the Interview score.

Question 16: Which scholarships does PUCIT offer?

Answer: PUCIT offers merit and need based scholarships every year from the University of the Punjab and its own resources to deserving students. Moreover, the HEC and Government of the Punjab also announce need–based HEC and PEEF scholarships every year.
